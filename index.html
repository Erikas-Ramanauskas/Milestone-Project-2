Simple but dificult

Tedoku is a mix between Tetris and Sudoku with drag and drop functionality. Your goal is fill the vertical or horizontal lines as well as 3x3 squares like usual Sudoku 1-9 numbers but using a shapes resemblign traditional Tetris game.

And just like in tetris once you fill up the row the blocks are gone alowing space for a new shapes as well as rewarding a well deserved points.

Run out of space and its a Game-Over! Or as Gamers says these days GG!!!

Gameplay

Gameplay is quote simplistic but there a few things to keep in mind.

  1. Drag the pieces from outside of game board to the play area and drop them as you see fit.
  2. Match 9 tiles in either of row, column or 3x3 square matching game grid.
  3. Once you macth the tiles you will open up more space and earn points!
  4. Avoid making gaps that will be later hard to fill up.
  5. You are rewarded more points for making combination clears of more than single set of 9 tiles.
  6. While doing combinations you will also get rewarded rotation points alowing you to rotate the shapes.
  7. Rotation buttons rotates both shapes same direction, so plan cerefuly before you click!
  8. As a game progresses there is higher chance for more dificult shapes to apear making the game incresingly harder.
  9. Play better every time to improve you highscores!!!

Dificulity settings

Few standarts across all game modes

  • Every tile destroyed rewars 1 point or in other words one group rewards 9 points
  • While destroying a combination player is rewarded for each group even if they cross path. Combination of 2 will reward 18 points regardles if they cross path as in first example above
  • Combination also adds aditional 50% points

  • Easy
    • Combination also rewards player with rotation points if 2 or more sets are destroyed in single turn
    • Player always starts easy mode with 3 rotation points
  • Medium
    • Medium game mode have higher chance for dificult shapes to show up
    • Minimum of 3 combination groups are required to earn rotation point
    • Game starts with 2 rotation points
  • Hard
    • Hard game mode have highest chance for dificult shapes to show up
    • With this mode at least 4 combnation groups are required to earn rotation point
    • Game starts with 1 rotation point
